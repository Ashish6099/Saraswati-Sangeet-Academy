About the Guitar Course

Our guitar course is designed to teach students the art of playing the guitar, one of the most popular and versatile instruments in the world. Whether you're a beginner or have some experience, our expert instructors will guide you through the fundamentals and advanced techniques of guitar playing.

Course Details

In our guitar course, you'll learn:

  • Proper posture and hand positioning
  • Chord progressions and strumming patterns
  • Reading guitar tabs and musical notation
  • Rhythm and timing
  • Scales and arpeggios
  • Improvisation and expression

Demo Classes

We offer free demo classes every weekend for those interested in trying out our guitar course. These classes provide a glimpse into our teaching methods and allow you to experience the joy of playing the guitar firsthand.

Registration is required due to limited availability. Contact us to reserve your spot for the demo classes.

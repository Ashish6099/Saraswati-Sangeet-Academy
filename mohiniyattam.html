About the Mohiniyattam Dance Course

Experience the grace and elegance of Mohiniyattam, a classical dance form originating from the Indian state of Kerala. Our Mohiniyattam dance course celebrates the traditional artistry of this ancient dance style while incorporating modern techniques and interpretations.

Expert Instructors

Learn from highly skilled Mohiniyattam dancers who are passionate about preserving and promoting this cultural heritage.

Expressive Movements

Master the art of storytelling through expressive facial expressions, graceful hand gestures, and intricate footwork.

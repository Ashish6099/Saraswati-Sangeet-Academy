About the Flute Course

Our flute course is designed to teach students the art of playing the flute, one of the most versatile and melodic instruments in the world. Whether you're a beginner or have some experience, our expert instructors will guide you through the fundamentals and advanced techniques of flute playing.

Course Details

In our flute course, you'll learn:

  • Proper posture and embouchure
  • Fingering techniques
  • Reading musical notation
  • Rhythm and timing
  • Scales and arpeggios
  • Improvisation and expression

Demo Classes

We offer free demo classes every weekend for those interested in trying out our flute course. These classes provide a glimpse into our teaching methods and allow you to experience the joy of playing the flute firsthand.

Registration is required due to limited availability. Contact us to reserve your spot for the demo classes.

About the Harmonium Course

Our harmonium course is designed to teach students the art of playing the harmonium, a beautiful and soulful instrument commonly used in Indian classical music. Whether you're a beginner or have some experience, our expert instructors will guide you through the fundamentals and advanced techniques of harmonium playing.

Course Details

In our harmonium course, you'll learn:

  • Proper sitting posture and hand position
  • Understanding of scales and ragas
  • Playing techniques for different compositions
  • Accompaniment skills for vocal and instrumental music
  • Improvisation and expression

Demo Classes

We offer free demo classes every weekend for those interested in trying out our harmonium course. These classes provide a glimpse into our teaching methods and allow you to experience the joy of playing the harmonium firsthand.

Registration is required due to limited availability. Contact us to reserve your spot for the demo classes.

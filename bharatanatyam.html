About Bharatnatyam

Bharatnatyam is one of the oldest classical dance forms of India, originating in the temples of Tamil Nadu. It is known for its intricate footwork, expressive hand gestures, and graceful movements that tell captivating stories from Hindu mythology.

Course Details

Our Bharatnatyam dance course offers a comprehensive learning experience for students of all levels. Our experienced instructors will guide you through the fundamentals of this classical dance form, including:

  • Basic postures and stances
  • Intricate footwork patterns
  • Hand gestures (mudras)
  • Abhinaya (expression)
  • Theoretical knowledge of Bharatnatyam

Demo Classes

To give you a taste of our Bharatnatyam dance course, we offer free demo classes every weekend. These classes will provide you with an overview of the dance form and allow you to experience the teaching style of our instructors.

Demo classes are open to all age groups and levels of experience. Registration is required due to limited availability. Please contact us for more information and to reserve your spot.

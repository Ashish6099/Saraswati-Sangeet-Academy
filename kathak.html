About the Kathak Dance Course

Our Kathak dance course is designed to teach students the art of Kathak, one of the eight classical Indian dance forms. Kathak is known for its intricate footwork, graceful movements, and expressive storytelling. Whether you're a beginner or have some experience, our expert instructors will guide you through the fundamentals and advanced techniques of Kathak dance.

Expert Instructors

Learn from experienced Kathak dancers who are passionate about teaching and preserving this beautiful art form.

Live Music

Experience the joy of dancing to live music played by talented musicians specializing in Indian classical music.
